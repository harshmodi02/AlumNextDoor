Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

Are mentors as good as consultants?

Here are the testimonials. We’ve been doing this for 2 years and we are only growing. Mentors, specifically targeted mentors, basis your profile can deliver curated insights more valuable than a generic consultant. More than decorating your application with high – fi vocabulary and sentences, our focus is to create a compelling story from your resume for the admission committee.

What are the services I get?

When you’re still thinking to apply to a school –
Your mentor can review your profile and give inputs on your chances of getting through or the areas where you need to build it more.

When you have written your first draft of the essay -
Your mentor can review it and discuss the gaps with you on a call.

When you have got an interview call –
Your mentor can conduct mock interviews over a video call.

How much does it cost?

As a platform we take no charges or commissions from applicants or mentors.

Mentors charge a negotiable price which is a fraction of the charges of similar services out there making us truly value for money!

We help in negotiating the structure of support (no. of reviews / interviews) and the price between you and your mentor. We also keep your payment in escrow until the mentor delivers on their promise.
